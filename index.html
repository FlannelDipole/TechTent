Trans Tech Tent




The Trans Tech Tent is a small collective of tech enthusiasts in South Wales

We deliver workshops on tech skills like finding your feet with technology, using social media effectively, and staying safe online.

We also help repair, and refurbish equipment for the community. We accept hardware donations!


We run hands-on technical workshops that are open to anyone, regardless of their skill level, we cover topics like:

  • Working in Tech

  • Digital Forensics

  • Programming and Design

  • Right to Repair



Follow us on one of our socials for more info as and when it happens!
